What is the mass percent?
The mass percentage is a representation of the concentration of an element in a compund or component in a mixture.
How to calculate mass percent?
To calculate mass percent you should use the following formula:
wi = (mi / mtot) * 100
where:
wi = mass fraction
mi = mass
mtot = total mass
Example: Calculate the mass percent of 11g of caustic soda dissolved in 170g of water.
wi = (mi / mtot) * 100
wi = (1 / (170 + 11)) * 100
wi = 60.77%
Why use mass percent?
The mass percent is used as a way to express the concentration or describing the component in a specific mixture. The solution composition can be described in mass percentage which tells the mass of solute that is in a specific mass of solution.
